# Product Trial Readiness Audit

Date: 2026-06-08
Scope: AI trading education learning gym, small-scope user trial only.

## Verdict

The product is close to a small-scope user trial, but it is not commercial-production ready.

- Trial readiness: about 97% for a bounded user trial.
- Commercial production readiness: about 30%.
- Required boundary: educationOnly true, productionReady false.
- Prohibited positioning: no stock recommendation, no live buy/sell signal, no return or win-rate promise, no broker connection, no auto-trading, no real-money trading instruction.

## What A User Should Understand In The First Session

This product is for people who are learning to read K-line structure and review their own decision process. It is not for people looking for stock picks, real trades, broker actions, or proof that a strategy makes money.

After three practice passes, the user should be able to:

1. Write one complete training plan.
2. Name one invalidation or recognition point.
3. Complete one replay without using future candles as evidence.

The first aha moment should be: "It caught the exact habit that makes my chart reading unreliable."

## Current Implementation Evidence

- User-track selection: `app.js` `learnerTrackOptions`, visible in the homepage trial outcome panel.
- Teaching flow: compact-by-default teaching intro with expandable examples and checklist.
- Chart teaching: `chartReadingGuide`, hidden-future mask, pullback-to-range callout, prior-high and invalidation annotations.
- Training flow state: `training-flow-state.js` controls read/choose/write/review stage metadata.
- Training answer helper: `data-plan-helper="example"` and `trainingPlanExample()` provide a safe education-only sample answer; `data-plan-helper="clear"` lets users restart without guessing format.
- Aha and AI review: `buildSharpDiagnosis` and `coachSharpDiagnosis` show concrete process mistakes.
- Replay link: `replayTrainingBridge` ties replay back to the just-completed training scenario.
- Backtest literacy: `backtest-plain-lesson` and `backtest-metric-plain-grid` translate metrics into everyday language.
- Runtime boundary: `readiness-boundary-grid` states mock/demo providers, `productionReady:false`, missing licensed data, missing real LLM audit, and education-only limits.
- Public preview seed: `npm.cmd run seed:public-preview` generated 4 approved public-preview education scenarios and records source constraints, `educationOnly:true`, and `productionReady:false`.

## Trial Go / No-Go

Go for a small, clearly bounded user trial if:

- The trial link runs locally or in a configured deployment.
- Users understand this is an education training product.
- Mock/demo AI and demo/public-preview data labels stay visible.
- Feedback is collected only about learning flow and clarity.

No-go for commercial production until:

- Licensed historical market data is contracted.
- Historical news and sentiment data rights are reviewed.
- Real LLM provider path is tested with keys, compliance filters, fallback, and audit logs.
- Scenario bank is much larger and reviewed.
- Deployment, privacy, data retention, and operational support are productionized.

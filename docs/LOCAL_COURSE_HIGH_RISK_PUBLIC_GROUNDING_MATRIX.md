## Completion Rule

This matrix maps high-risk lessons to public/Wikipedia grounding for reviewer use. It clears the mapping task, not the release gate: every lesson still needs human public-grounding review, originality review, and separate approval before learner-facing use.

## Boundary

High-risk public grounding matrix is internal reviewer evidence planning only. Wikipedia and public sources support terminology, taxonomy, attribution-aware references, and original education rewrites; they do not approve learner-facing release, make private PDFs public citations, provide stock recommendations, live signals, return promises, broker workflows, automation, or real-money guidance.
